What Is a Digital Twin?
A Digital Twin is a dynamic digital representation of a physical asset, system, or environment that continuously integrates data from design models, operational systems, IoT, and AI analytics.
Digital Twins enable organizations to:
Visualize assets in real time
Simulate scenarios before making decisions
Predict failures and optimize performance
Reduce cost, risk, and carbon footprint
Improve collaboration across stakeholders
